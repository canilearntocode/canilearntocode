    How to use this site:

    The curriculum section provides a curated list of learning resources, for various topics in web development, software engineering and computer science. These books, courses and videos will help you go from total beginner to junior level developer.

    The tutorials section includes hands-on, project-based tutorials on a variety of tools and technologies for developing websites.

    The blog includes notes on my own learning experience entering the field of professional web development, links to other useful articles and content and whatever random stuff I feel like posting.

    This site's mission is to provide a free resource for beginner and self taught programmers to develop professional level skills.
